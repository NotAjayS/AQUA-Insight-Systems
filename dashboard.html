About our Web-Application

The Smart Water Monitoring System is a web-based application developed to help users track and manage their water usage efficiently. It provides real-time insights into daily, monthly, and yearly water consumption through a clean and interactive dashboard. The system is designed to detect abnormal usage patterns and send alerts in case of potential leaks or wastage.
