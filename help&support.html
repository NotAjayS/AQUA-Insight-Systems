Understanding the Dashboard

          The dashboard is the central control hub of your Smart Water Monitoring System, designed to offer users a seamless and insightful experience. It provides real-time data visualization, allowing you to monitor key metrics such as daily water usage, tank levels, and motor activity with precision. The top overview tiles reflect summary statistics for today, monthly, and yearly usage, giving you instant clarity on your water consumption patterns. Interactive charts and animated indicators provide detailed breakdowns of consumption trends, helping you identify peak usage periods and take informed decisions to conserve water. The motor status tile updates in real-time and highlights whether the system is actively pumping, idle, or requires attention due to an error or disconnection.

        Alerts appear as notifications and are color-coded based on severity, ensuring you never miss a critical update. Additionally, the system logs every action and event, enabling transparency and traceability for technical or maintenance purposes. The sidebar is designed for intuitive navigation, granting quick access to booking, settings, and support panels. Each element within the dashboard is responsive and touch-optimized, allowing smooth usage across mobile devices and desktops. Dynamic icons animate subtly when changes occur, drawing attention only when necessary without overwhelming the user. Historical data can be viewed using the timeline scroll, offering week-over-week and month-over-month comparisons. Advanced settings enable custom notifications, sensor calibration, and smart threshold adjustments, putting you in full control. The dashboard also supports multiple user roles, so agents and administrators can have access based on their permissions. Whether you're monitoring a residential setup or managing a community water system, the dashboard adapts to your scale. All modules are synchronized with the cloud, ensuring your data is always secure and accessible. With thoughtful use of colors, animations, and real-time updates, the dashboard not only looks modern but enhances productivity. It eliminates the need to manually check sensors or tanks, reducing operational load significantly.

Common Troubleshooting Tips

       Troubleshooting common issues in your Smart Water Monitoring System ensures smooth operation and long-term performance. One of the most frequent problems users encounter is the motor not starting; in such cases, verify the power supply and make sure the Wi-Fi connection is stable and active. A blinking red icon on the dashboard usually indicates disconnection or a hardware fault.

      Restarting the mobile or desktop app often resolves minor software glitches and refreshes sensor data. Ensure the sensor cables are firmly connected and not exposed to moisture or corrosion. If water level readings appear stuck or inaccurate, try gently cleaning the sensor tip and reinitializing it from the settings panel. Network lags or router resets may cause temporary delays in real-time updates—wait a few moments or reconnect to refresh the data. Be mindful of app permissions; location and network access must be enabled for certain features to function correctly. If your dashboard isn't loading properly, clear browser/app cache or update to the latest version.

          For users facing issues with booking or alerts not showing, check for pending software updates and apply them promptly. Resetting the motor module occasionally helps recalibrate the system. Always cross-check your configuration settings if thresholds or notifications behave unusually. If you're receiving false alerts, recalibrate the affected sensors via the advanced options in the settings panel. In multi-user environments, ensure no other device has overridden your current settings. During heavy rainfall or power cuts, protect your sensors and circuits from water ingress and surges using insulated casing. If the system becomes unresponsive, switch off power for 10 seconds and reboot safely. Refer to logs in the dashboard to trace event history and pinpoint irregular behavior. For deeper technical issues, consult the embedded help manual or reach out via the support form for expert guidance. Regular maintenance and periodic cleaning of sensors go a long way in avoiding recurring problems. The system is built with auto-diagnostics, so any major faults will be reported promptly with suggested actions

  • Check if the power supply is connected properly.
  • Ensure Wi-Fi connectivity is stable and strong.
  • Restart the device to clear temporary glitches.
  • Inspect sensor placement and cleanliness.
  • Update the app regularly for smooth performance.

FAQ's

Why is my water usage unusually high?▼
       Check for leakages in pipes, faucets, tanks, or underground lines that may be causing unnoticed water loss. Even small drips can lead to significant wastage over time. Use the dashboard’s flow analysis tools to detect continuous usage during inactive hours. If usage spikes persist without known activity, it's likely a hidden leak that needs urgent repair.
How to reset the motor remotely?▼
      Go to the Motor tab on the dashboard and select the Reset option. Confirm the reset action when prompted. Make sure your device has a stable internet connection during the reset process to avoid interruption. If the reset fails, wait for a few minutes and try again or check the connectivity of your control unit.
What to do if alerts are not working?▼
       Ensure that your phone's app notifications are enabled under system settings. Check your network connectivity as alerts depend on real-time synchronization. You can also review alert preferences in the app’s settings tab. If the issue persists, try logging out and back into the app.
Can I schedule motor operations?▼
       Yes, the app allows flexible scheduling of motor start and stop times. Navigate to the Schedule tab and choose your preferred time slots. You can set recurring patterns for daily or weekly cycles. Ensure the system remains powered and connected during those slots.
How to contact support directly?▼
       You can reach us via the Contact page on the website or email at smartwater@support.com. A live chat option is available within the app during business hours. Our team typically responds within 24 hours. For urgent issues, use the "Call Now" button if visible.
